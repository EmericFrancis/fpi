Re Issue an FPI summary

People call us when something goes wrong - e.g. i didn't see the summary; i dodn;t manage to take a copy it. We want to re-issue them a copy of the summary (one possible solution)

The current process involves a lot of internal people/teams; the user wants to get their situation resolved immediately (while on the phone)

A good principle is: for our support staff to use the same system the user has to use - so they understand what the use has done, and also gain familiarity with the service - better to suppprt other tyoes of call

For the user though - they want their outcome as quickly as possible. And we would also like to spend as little time as possible resolving the user enquiry

Some related problems / things we know

  • Users don't know / cant describe well which service/product they are using
    It's hard for them (and us to ask) whether its FPI, FaP, or ot a lesser degree property alert
  • people find it hard to describvle what they were doing / looking for. especially as:
  • people don't know HMLR terminology - title, title number, tenure, freehold and leasehold
  • sometimes the title summary they paid for isn't the one they wanted/needed

A tool that showed what service people have looked at/bought things from would aid problem resolution

Proto 1

Call Centre staff just use the current system - having ascertained from the user, that they are using FPI, and got the address and tenure form the user

Call centre staff have a role that means they donl;t have to pay (and maybe also not sign in) - so the system does all the auditing and logging

Proto 2

Call Centre staff have a page which they enter the users account name - e.g. their email address or username - we show which system they used and when, and the address and title number of the thing they bought.

Call centre staff then just use the current system - either as is (postcode/address search), or we build a route straight to the summary page - so they can download a copy
