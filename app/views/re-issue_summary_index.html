Re Issue an FPI summary

People call us when something goes wrong - e.g. i didn't see the summary; i didn't manage to save it/take a copy it. We want to re-issue them a copy of the summary (one possible solution)

The current process involves a lot of internal people/teams and time; the user wants to get their situation resolved immediately

A good principle is: for our support staff to use the same system the user has to use - so they understand what the use has done, and also gain familiarity with the service - better to support other tyoes of call

For the user though - they want their outcome as quickly as possible. And we would also like to spend as little time as possible resolving the user enquiry

Some related problems / things we know

  • Users don't know and/or can't describe well, which service/product they are using
    It's hard for them (and us to ask) to say whether its FPI, FaP
  • people find it hard to describe what they were doing / looking for. especially as:
    • people don't know HMLR terminology - title, title number, tenure, freehold and leasehold
  • sometimes the title summary they paid for isn't the one they wanted/needed
  • we don't automatically monitor and record re-issuing of docs. We rely on manual recording
  • FPI - we don't think there are many calls when people fail to see the summary; but when we implement the download function, we expect calls to rise (similar to FAP calls)

A tool that showed what service people have looked at/bought things from would aid problem resolution

option 1

Call Centre staff just use the current system - having ascertained from the user, that they are using FPI, and got the address and tenure form the user

Call centre staff have a role that means they don't have to pay* (and maybe also not sign in) - so the system does all the auditing and logging
* or give them a prepaid cc (so we don't get worried about GPC cards and all the processes that entails)

option 2 (see prototype demo)

Call Centre staff have a page on which they enter the users account name - e.g. their email address or username - we show which system they used and when, and the details of the thing they bought.

Call centre staff can then reissue the PDF version - send it straight to their email while user is on the phone (so user can check its received), or staff can download (and print it) and reissue it by post

This doesn't allow (in first iterations) to reissue Find a Property docs, but does give support staff the details of what needs re-issuing, so they can use FaP to generate one
