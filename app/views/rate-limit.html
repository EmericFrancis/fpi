Rate limiting the search

Need: License Obligations - Ordnance Survey

In order to protect Ordnance Survey intellecutal property / comply with their license terms we'll rate limiting the number of addresses that can be consumed

This page prototype does not explain or mimic the implemented rate limiting rules

Aims:

  • a person should not be affected by the rate limits
  • prevent automated harvesting of addresses
  • monitor the behaviour of searches to improve the system
  • If a person does hit one of the limits - give them a forward journey - help them complete their task - whilst we are improving the rules
  • We have thought of scenarios where humans could hit the limit - see the trello card (authorised access needed)

Initial ideas for messages/forward journeys for people

Thoughts:

  • design for everyone - non javascript & javascript enabled
  • keep it as simple as possible for now - MVP
  • tell them/help them do the 'obvious thing' - what you would tell someone - 'refresh the page in x seconds'
  • clear, actionable content
    • don't explain the rules, or 'rate limiting'
    • solve the problem automatically and/or
    • help them do what we need them to do - refresh the page
    • don't cause unintentional negative behaviour / worry
