pick the property i'm interested in

Warning - some of the examples below are tailored around a secific solution/MVP.

The problem (user task) is find the right property (pick the right title record). Building a search based on AddressBase is one potential way of solving the problem. See the 'MVP Candidate' document (google drive) for technology agnostic solutions to the user problem in FPI service

In the MVP Candidate document, we recommend two candidates:

  1. everything HMLR has in a postcode (all titles)
  2. every title HMLR has for the postal addresses in a postcode (not land)
the pages/examples below are primarily focused on candidate 2.

If we're building the 'LLC Address component' tech solution - these pages explore/show things that need considering for the Find Property Information service - what to do for the user. Problems the user will have, and based on what we've learnt, suggested solutions in Find property information (FPI).
