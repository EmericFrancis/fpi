What is does, that's new

  • a result for every address in a postcode (whether we have something or not) (what the Royal Mail would show)
  • search result is the 'postal address' (addressbase delivery point address)
  • where we dont have anything available for a postal address - explain why/what to do
  • only postcode search
  • new - 'page per property address' - contains a link to each title at that address*
    * what we know we've got, and what is available in this service
  • 100 results per search - increasing from the current 50. And it's 100 addresses, not just 50 titles
