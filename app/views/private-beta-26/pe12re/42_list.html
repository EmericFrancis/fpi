What's the difference between freehold and leasehold?

If you own the freehold for land or a building you own it outright - it's yours until you sell it or give it away. Most houses are freehold.

If you own the leasehold for land or a building you own it for a fixed period of time. Most flats are leasehold - the blocks they're in are usually freehold. All leasehold properties will have a freehold title.
